Breakfast Tacos

Description

A delicious meal that anyone can make

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Flour tortilla
  • Butter
  • 5 large eggs
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Avocado
  • Green onions
  • Hot sauce
  • Everything bagel seasoning

Steps

  1. Spread layer of sausage evenly over tortilla
  2. Melt butter on nonstick skillet and add eggs
  3. Place tortillas on the griddle meat side down on a large medium-high heat skillet
  4. Fold tacos in half and top with eggs, cheese, avocado, green onions, and a sprinkle of bagel seasoning
